DigitalBeauty Pro — Ideal Customer Profile

Strategic Targeting & Positioning Document

1) ICP × Job-to-be-Done Matrix

Not all users who find the product interesting will pay. You need to identify who has:

  • frequent pain,
  • budget,
  • and decision-making authority.

Below is the real map, based on how these teams work today.

ICP 1 — Social Media Manager (fashion / beauty / luxury brands)

CONVERSION: HIGHEST
$39–$99/month per seat

Job-to-be-Done

Wants to answer:

  • what are other brands doing right now
  • what's working
  • what should we do next week

Not in 3 months. Now.

How they work today

Real method:

  • follows 50–200 accounts manually
  • saves posts on Instagram
  • uses Notion / screenshots / Slack
  • produces manual reports

Hidden cost: 5–10 hours/week.

Why DigitalBeauty is better

Signals + Explore replaces:

  • manual scrolling
  • manual collection
  • initial interpretation

Agents replaces:

  • weekly reports
  • benchmark analysis

→ This ICP is your best initial wedge

ICP 2 — Creative Director / Art Director

CONVERSION: HIGH
$99–$300/month

Job-to-be-Done

Wants to know:

  • how is the aesthetic language evolving
  • what's becoming cliché
  • what's emerging

Not looking for data. Looking for creative advantage.

Why this ICP is powerful

They are trend-sensitive and have strong continuous need. And most importantly, they influence high-value decisions.

ICP 3 — Founder / CMO of Challenger Brands

CONVERSION: HIGHEST

This ICP is underestimated.

Founders of DTC / emerging brands:

  • need intelligence
  • don't have large teams
  • make decisions directly

DigitalBeauty becomes: their competitive radar

→ This ICP can become one of the most profitable.

ICP 4 — Agencies (social, creative, branding)

CONVERSION: EXTREMELY HIGH
$200–$1000+/month

Why

They use the product for:

  • many clients
  • many brands
  • daily use

→ This ICP is often the most profitable in SaaS intelligence.

ICP 5 — Enterprise Brands

CONVERSION: MEDIUM
$5k–$50k/year

Reality

Slower sales. But very high ACV.

This is a next step, not initial.

ICP PRIORITY ORDER (Recommended)

Launch order:

  1. Social media managers (challenger brands)
  2. Agencies
  3. Creative directors
  4. Founder brands
  5. Enterprise

2) The 3 Possible Strategic Positionings (Choose One)

This is the most critical point. Success depends more on positioning than product.

POSITIONING A — Social Intelligence Platform

"Monitor what brands are doing"

PROS

  • easy to understand

CONS

  • saturated market
  • competition with:
    • Brandwatch
    • Meltwater
    • Dash Hudson

→ NOT recommended as primary positioning.

POSITIONING B — Creative Intelligence Platform ⭐ RECOMMENDED

"Understand how aesthetic communication is evolving"

PROS

  • much more differentiated
  • much weaker competition
  • aligned with your DNA

→ THIS is the best initial positioning.

POSITIONING C — Agentic Intelligence Platform

"Agents that analyze and act on creative data"

PROS

  • huge future potential

CONS

  • today too new to be primary positioning

Must be phase two, not phase one.

CLEAR RECOMMENDATION

Primary positioning: Creative Intelligence Platform

Secondary positioning: Agentic Creative Intelligence Platform
